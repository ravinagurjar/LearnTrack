About LearnTrack

LearnTrack is a personal learning journal designed to help students record their daily progress, reflect on concepts, and build consistent learning habits.


Why LearnTrack?

While learning programming and technical skills, it is easy to forget what we studied or lose track of improvement. LearnTrack provides a simple way to maintain a record of learning sessions and revisit previous knowledge.


Features

  • Record daily learning sessions
  • Track study time and confidence level
  • Maintain a personal learning journal
  • Reflect on doubts and improvements

Technologies Used

HTML5 • CSS3 • Flask • Python • SQLite
